About Us

Kool Cakes Bakery was formerly known as Paul’s Bakers, which started in 1994 under the ownership of Kulwinder Paul Kumar & his wife Salinder Kaur. Paul's skill and proficiency in the art of modelling gum paste flowers and decorations are exemplary. He attained his masters degree in cake decoration as well as diplomas in cake decorating from the US.
